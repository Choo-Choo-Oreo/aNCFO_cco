[h1]Presentation[/h1]
This is a fantasy mod for HoI4 based off my own stories and documents. I am a solo dev with some support from friends. Though I will not this doesn't follow common fantasy and JRR Tolkien fantasy tropes. Due to me being a solo dev I don't have many events with written descriptions but I hope the titles are clear enough to figure out what is happening! Please join the discord and tell me about your game as its important to help me develop the mod quicker.

[h1]Features[/h1]
+ New custom map.
+ Expanded technology trees.
+ New technology trees.

[h1]Major Update Changes[/h1]
- Resized the Map (25% more land usage, reduced water surface)
- Added Species, Ethnos, and Heritage map systems
- New multi-layered Coring mechanics replacing the standard system
- Integration of Naval Capacity and updated Battalion Capacity mechanics
- New Formable Nation for the Northeast Dwarf States
