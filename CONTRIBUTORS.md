## 📜 Role Definitions

### **Artists**
Individuals responsible for the visual identity of the mod. This includes flags, portraits, 3D models, and UI elements. They retain ownership of the raw and compiled graphical files they produce.

### **Assistants**
Individuals who provide secondary support, such as writing localization strings, balancing data tables, or researching lore. They own the specific text or data configurations they contribute.

### **Collaborators**
Individuals who work on major logic or system overhauls alongside the Author. They retain ownership of the specific scripts or mechanics they independently develop.
